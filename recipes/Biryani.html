Biryani

Biryani is an Indian food. Which is made from chicken and rice.

It is very spicy and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Mustard-oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Indian Spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add some oil into rice-cooker let it becomes hot.
  2. Add onions and garlic and let it golden-brown.
  3. Add chicken, rice and spices and mix them and fry.
  4. Close the lid of cooker and heat for 10 minutes.
  5. The biryani is ready!
