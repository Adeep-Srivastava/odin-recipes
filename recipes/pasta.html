Pasta

Pasta is the most famous continental food. It is very tasty!

Ingredients

  • Raw-Pasta
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Veggies
  • Spices

Steps

  1. First boil raw pasta for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add some butter into a pan.
  3. Fry veggies with spices into the pan.
  4. Now add boiled pasta into fried veggies.
  5. Cook for 1 minute and pasta is ready.
